## Bio
Nick Hook is a New York-based producer, DJ, and artist whose credits include Run The Jewels, Azealia Banks, 21 Savage, Young Thug, Grand Theft Auto, and Red Bull Music Academy. A gold-record winner and co-executive producer of RTJ Cuatro, he is known for his raw analog energy and genre-defying live sets. From Boiler Room to Sónar and global club stages, Hook unites worlds through sound, with roots in The Space Pit and inspiration from Brooklyn to Medellín.
